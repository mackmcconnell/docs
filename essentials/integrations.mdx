All integrations can be managed from your [Integrations section](https://app.markix.com/settings/integrations).

## Slack
For scheduled, recurring queries, Markix can send updated results directly to your team in Slack. 
This integration will add a bot to your workspace. You can configure this bot to enter any channel.
